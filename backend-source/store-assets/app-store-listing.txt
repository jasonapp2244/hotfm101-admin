------ DESCRIPTION ------

Hot 101.5 Radio brings your favorite station straight to your iPhone. Stream live radio anywhere, stay on top of music news, enter contests to win amazing prizes, and discover events and deals happening right in your neighborhood.

LIVE STREAMING
Tune in to Hot FM 101.5 with crystal-clear audio streaming. Listen while you commute, work out, or relax. Background playback keeps the music going while you use other apps. Choose dark or light theme to match your style.

MUSIC NEWS
Stay current with the latest music news, artist updates, and entertainment stories. From breaking headlines to features, we keep you informed about the artists and music you love.

CONTESTS & GIVEAWAYS
Enter exclusive contests for a chance to win concert tickets, meet-and-greets, gift cards, and more. Push notification alerts make sure you never miss a chance to win.

EVENTS
Discover concerts, station events, and community happenings near you. RSVP directly from the app, see full event details, and get reminders so you never miss out.

LOCAL DEALS
Enable location services to unlock deals from businesses near you. Find savings on dining, shopping, and entertainment right in your neighborhood.

VIDEO & PODCASTS
Watch video broadcasts, artist interviews, behind-the-scenes content, and podcast episodes via YouTube. Access exclusive station content whenever you want.

SHOUTOUTS
Record and submit a 30-second video shoutout to be featured on the station. Send birthday wishes, dedicate a song, or show your love for Hot 101.5.

PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
Get real-time alerts for breaking news, new contests, upcoming events, local deals, and station updates.

Download now and take Hot 101.5 everywhere you go.
