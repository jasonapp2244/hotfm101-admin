====== PRIVACY PRACTICES OVERVIEW ======

Does your app track users?
No. The app does not track users across other companies' apps or websites
for advertising or advertising measurement purposes.

Yes.

Privacy Nutrition Label URL: https://hotfm1015.com/privacy-policy


====== DATA LINKED TO YOUR IDENTITY ======

The following data is collected and linked to your identity:

1. Contact Info
   - Name (used for: App Functionality)
   - Email Address (used for: App Functionality)
   - Phone Number (used for: App Functionality - contest winner contact, optional)

2. User Content
   - Audio Data (used for: App Functionality - shoutout submissions)
   - Video Data (used for: App Functionality - shoutout submissions)
   - Other User Content (used for: App Functionality - contest entries, RSVPs)


====== DATA NOT LINKED TO YOUR IDENTITY ======

The following data is collected but not linked to your identity:

1. Location
   - Precise Location (used for: App Functionality - location-based deals)
   - Coarse Location (used for: App Functionality - location-based deals)

2. Usage Data
   - Product Interaction (used for: Analytics)

3. Diagnostics
   - Crash Data (used for: App Functionality - stability improvement)
   - Performance Data (used for: App Functionality)

4. Identifiers
   - Device ID (used for: App Functionality - push notifications)


====== DATA NOT COLLECTED ======

The following data types are NOT collected:

- Financial Info (payment info, credit card info)
- Health & Fitness
- Sensitive Info
- Contacts
- Browsing History
- Search History
- Purchases
- Emails or Messages
