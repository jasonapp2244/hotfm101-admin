Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026 | Last Updated: April 6, 2026

AppScrub ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Hot 101.5 Radio mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the App. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture (optional).
  • Contest Entries: When you enter contests, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and date of birth to verify eligibility and deliver prizes.
  • Event RSVPs: When you RSVP to events, we collect your name and email address.
  • Shoutout Video Submissions: When you submit a shoutout video (up to 30 seconds), we collect the video recording, which may include your likeness, voice, and any content you choose to share. This requires access to your device camera and microphone.
  • Communications: When you contact us for support or feedback, we collect the content of your messages and any information you voluntarily provide.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and app version.
  • Usage Data: Listening history, articles read, features used, time spent in the App, and interaction patterns.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens used to deliver push notifications when you opt in to receive them.
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed, app crashes, and other system activity.

1.3 Location Information

With your explicit consent, we collect your precise or approximate geographic location to:

  • Provide location-based advertisements and deals from local businesses.
  • Show relevant events happening near you.
  • Deliver region-specific content and promotions.

You may disable location services at any time through your device settings. Disabling location services may limit certain features of the App.

1.4 Camera and Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device camera and microphone solely for the purpose of recording shoutout video submissions. This access is requested only when you choose to record a shoutout and is never activated without your explicit action. We do not access your camera or microphone for any other purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

3.1 Google Firebase

We use Firebase for authentication, cloud database, analytics, crash reporting, cloud messaging (push notifications), and remote configuration. Firebase may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data. For more information, see the Firebase Privacy Policy.

3.2 YouTube API Services

The App uses the YouTube API to display video broadcasts and podcast content. By using features that access YouTube content, you are also bound by the Google Privacy Policy. YouTube may collect data about your video viewing activity. You can manage your YouTube privacy settings at Google Security Settings.

3.3 Advertising Partners

We work with third-party advertising networks to display location-based advertisements. These partners may use cookies, device identifiers, and location data to serve relevant ads. We do not share personally identifiable information with advertisers without your consent.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating the App, conducting our business, or servicing you, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Contest Sponsors: With contest sponsors solely for the purpose of prize fulfillment, and only with your consent at the time of entry.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of Rights: When we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, in which case you will be notified via email or a prominent notice in the App.
  • Public Shoutouts: Shoutout video submissions you submit may be displayed publicly within the App and on associated broadcast platforms. Do not include any information in your shoutout that you do not wish to be made public.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
  • Contest Entries: Retained for 12 months after the conclusion of the contest for verification and legal compliance purposes.
  • Shoutout Videos: Retained for as long as you maintain an active account, or until you request deletion. After deletion request, videos are removed within 30 days.
  • Usage and Analytics Data: Retained in aggregated, anonymized form for up to 24 months for analytical purposes.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Retained until you uninstall the App or revoke notification permissions.
  • Location Data: Not stored persistently. Location is used in real-time to serve relevant content and is not retained after the session ends.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL.
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest.
  • Firebase Authentication for secure account management.
  • Regular security assessments and updates.
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 All Users

  • Account Deletion: You may delete your account at any time through the App settings or by contacting us at support@appscrub.com.
  • Push Notifications: You may opt out of push notifications through your device settings at any time.
  • Location Services: You may disable location access through your device settings.
  • Camera/Microphone: You may revoke camera and microphone permissions through your device settings.

7.2 Rights Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - EEA Residents

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit the processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data for certain purposes, including direct marketing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@appscrub.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7.3 Rights Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected, the categories of sources, the business purpose for collecting the data, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information. If this changes, we will provide a "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@appscrub.com or submit a request through the App. We will verify your identity before processing your request and respond within 45 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@appscrub.com. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under the applicable age, we will promptly delete that information.

Users between the ages of 13 and 17 must have parental or guardian consent to use the App. Contest participation may require participants to be 18 years or older, as specified in individual contest rules.

By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers in compliance with applicable law.
